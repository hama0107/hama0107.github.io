About Me

My name is Iryna, I'm in my early 20s, and I enjoy being creative. I am currently studying IMD at Algonquin College. I love photography because it allows me to freeze moments. I like to shoot on film, wait for the result, develop it, and then process and edit the pictures in Photoshop, adding more colours and meaning to them.

I also like painting with oil paints because it is a slow and thoughtful process. I like to mix colors, work with the texture of the canvas and gradually convey my mood and my own thoughts through the paints.
